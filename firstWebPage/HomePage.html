St. Mary's Higher Secondary School is a primary and secondary school in Thiruvananthapuram, India.
It is founded in 1940 by Archbishop Geevarghese Mar Ivanios. It is considered one of the largest schools
in Asia, with the total number of students exceeding 14,000.
